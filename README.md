🇳🇱 Nederlands Flashcard
A personal flashcard app I built to learn Dutch while working in the Netherlands. No installation needed — works like a native app on your phone.

→ Open App

Why I built this
Most flashcard apps are either too generic or too complicated. I needed something focused on workplace Dutch — technical terms, vendor conversations, daily small talk. So I made my own.
How to install on iPhone
Open the link in Safari → tap the Share button at the bottom → "Add to Home Screen". That's it. Opens full screen, no browser bar.
What's inside

180+ words across 10 topic blocks
Swipe right if you know it, left if you don't
Words you miss come back more often
Daily goal of 10 words with progress tracking
Reminder notifications at a time you set
Works offline
